Background of the project, and how to Load the Data

The main goal of this project is to track every person that lands on our site.  Additionally, we will track every time they click on a page. How long are they on each page? Where are they coming from? Marketing would really like to understand that journey so as to increase the conversions on our site.For us to best illustrate this, we will use a  Scatter chart to show us the Total SessionCnt and same day registration. We will also use a map to  show us where people are hitting our site from and what percentage is signing up.

Key Terms used  include:  

 PreUser- When a user hits any of our sites for the 1st time, their IP Address and Location information is captured and stored in our Database. If the user is directed from a marketing ad and this is their 1st time to the site, they will be tagged a marketing source. 

SessionCnt-  Number of unique users per day

LoginDay: Day of Login

PageCnt: Total pages touched in the session 

MinDate: First touch to the site

MaxDate: Last recorded touch to the site

TotalSessionSec: Total number of seconds recorded on site

TotalSessionMin: Total number of minutes recorded on site

UserID: If they registered on the site, this is their userid

RegistrationDate: If they registered on the site, this is their registration date

SameDayReg: If this session and registration happened on the same day
